spelling_table/3

spelling_table(Server::x_server(), Meta::x_term_meta(), UserParams::[term()]) -> x_table()

An iterator which returns all the spelling correction targets.

Valid fields are:
  • value;
  • freq - The frequency of each word.
The wdf field isn't meaningful. The records are sorted by the value field.
